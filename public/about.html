About Us

Move On was born from the recognition that fitness journeys are deeply intertwined with mental health. We're not just another fitness app, we're a community dedicated to creating genuine connections.

Why We Started

We witnessed too many peers struggle alone with fitness anxiety and mental health challenges. We realized that pairing beginners with supportive mentors could transform both physical and mental wellness journeys.

Our Commitment

Every feature is designed with mental wellness in mind. We prioritize creating safe spaces where vulnerability is strength, progress is celebrated, and every interaction builds genuine confidence and connection.

"Our vision for Move On is to create a community that serves not as an escape, but as a gathering of like-minded individuals forging genuine friendships built on mutual improvement."
- Move On Team
